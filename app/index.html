Traditional

Our bespoke range of furniture enables us, the designers, to turn your visions into a living reality. Custom made to your specification, from a hand painted kitchen to a solid walnut snooker room, we are able to transcend our craft into any part of your home.

Contemporary

Being on trend and utilising modern materials is key. We combine these two factors when creating contemporary designs and ensure our suppliers are market leaders in their field. This installs the piece of mind we desire, when sometimes millimetres are the difference between success and failure.

Interiors

From Carrara Italian marble slabs to Canadian Hemlock sauna's, Vellos Designs apply their expertise to creating spaces for any area of the home. We have grown a portfolio of tried and tested products that we utilise within our designs regularly. Our products are all supplied and fitted by our experienced staff.

Architectural

As an architectural interior design studio, we pride ourselves on the fact we can offer an all encompassing service, which many other design studio's lack. From extensions on Kensington town houses, to basement swimming pool installations, no project is too big or too small.
